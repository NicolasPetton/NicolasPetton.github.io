Pharo Books

Deep into Pharo

Deep into Pharo is the second volume of a series of books covering Pharo. Whereas the first volume, Pharo by Example, is intended for newcomers, this second volume covers deeper topics.

Deep into Pharo can be downloaded for free or ordered as a softcover copy.

Pharo By Example

Pharo by Example, intended for both students and developers, will guide you gently through the Pharo language and environment by means of a series of examples and exercises.

Pharo by Example can be downloaded for free or ordered as a softcover copy.

The Pharo CollaborActive Book

A collaboratively written book by the Pharo community. Read, use, add comments, extend and help improve it!
