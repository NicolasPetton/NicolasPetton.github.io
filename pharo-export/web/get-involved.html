Contributing to Pharo

In a giving mood? There are many ways to get involved!

Reporting issues

If you found a bug or issue with Pharo please let us know. You can report bugs on the issue tracker.

Note that all code submitted to the Pharo repositories is licensed under the MIT license.

The following video explains how to submit a bug fix:

How to create a bug fix for Pharo from dh83 on Vimeo.

The Pharo consortium

Companies can join the consortium. Its goal is to structure and build an umbrella to foster business around Pharo and to promote Pharo. By becoming a consortium member you help promoting Pharo, and support de day to day development of Pharo.

Pharo consortium members also get professional support.

Learn more.

The Pharo association

The Pharo association's goal is to let individuals support the promotion and development of Pharo. The association is a Loi de 1901 french association. The idea behind the association is that even as single person we can get an impact because we group together.

Learn more.
