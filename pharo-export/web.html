The live Programming environment

Pharo is a clean, innovative, opensource, purely object-oriented environment for MacOS X, Windows and Linux.

Why Pharo?

Pharo is an general-purpose language that includes a live-programming environment.

Rediscover your debugger

The Pharo environment includes a debugger unlike anything you've seen before. It allows you to step through code, restart the execution of methods, create methods on the fly, and much more!

Simple & powerful language

Public methods, protected attributes, single inheritance, and objects all the way down. No primitive types, no constructors, no generics, no interfaces, yet a powerful and elegant language.
