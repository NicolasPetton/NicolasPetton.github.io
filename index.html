About me.

I'm a Sotfware engineer, passionate about Lisp, Smalltalk and the Web. I split my time between Stockholm (Sweden) and Brest (France). Oh, and I also spend most of my days in Emacs.

Projects.

  • I'm also the author of SmalltalkHub, a code repository and project management application for Smalltalk
  • I hack/work on several Emacs packages: js2-refactor, seq.el, noccur.el, amd-mode, niflheim-emacs, telepathy.el and org-gnome.

  • I often hack my Emacs settings.
